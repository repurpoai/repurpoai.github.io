Refund Policy

Repurpo • Effective Date: 30 March 2026

This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be available for purchases of Repurpo subscriptions and related services offered by Repurpo.

1. General Policy

Because Repurpo is a digital software service with immediately accessible features and usage-based value, all sales are generally considered final once access has been granted, except where a refund is required by applicable law or expressly approved under this policy.

2. When We May Consider a Refund

We may, in our sole discretion, consider a refund in limited circumstances such as:

  • You were charged more than once for the same purchase due to a billing error.
  • You were charged after a valid cancellation due to a system error.
  • You purchased the wrong plan and contact us promptly before substantial use of the service.
  • A verified technical issue on our side made the paid service materially unavailable and we could not resolve it within a reasonable time.
  • A refund is required under applicable consumer protection law.

3. When Refunds Are Normally Not Available

Refunds are normally not available for:

  • Partial billing periods.
  • Change of mind after purchase.
  • Failure to cancel before the next renewal date.
  • Dissatisfaction with output style, preferences, or subjective expectations where the service was available and functional.
  • Account restrictions or suspension caused by violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Unused time, unused generations, or unused plan allowances.

4. Subscription Cancellations

If you cancel a subscription, the cancellation applies to future renewals. You will generally retain access to paid features until the end of the current billing period, and the current billing period will not normally be refunded unless required by law or expressly approved by us.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email repurpo.ai@gmail.com with the subject line Refund Request and include:

  • Your account email address
  • Order or invoice number
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the request
  • Any helpful screenshots or details

Please contact us as soon as possible after the charge. Requests made promptly are easier to review and resolve fairly.
